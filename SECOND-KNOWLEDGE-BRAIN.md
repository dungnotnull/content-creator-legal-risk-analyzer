## 1. Core Concepts & Frameworks
- **Copyright fair-use four-factor test (US) / fair dealing** — Use-permissibility analysis.
  1. Purpose and character of the use (commercial vs. transformative).
  2. Nature of the copyrighted work (factual vs. highly creative).
  3. Amount and substantiality of portion used.
  4. Effect of the use upon the potential market.
- **DMCA / platform content-ID frameworks** — Notice-and-takedown, counter-notice, safe-harbor, and automated content identification that affects monetization.
- **Creative Commons license matrix** — License-compatibility checks across CC0, BY, BY-SA, BY-NC, BY-ND, BY-NC-SA.
- **Trademark likelihood-of-confusion factors** — Strength of mark, similarity of goods/services, channels of trade, actual confusion, intent.
- **Right of publicity / personality rights** — Unauthorized commercial use of name, likeness, voice, or persona.
